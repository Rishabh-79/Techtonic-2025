About Us

The Cybernauts student association of the Department of Computer Science is dedicated to motivating students to explore the world of computer science, develop innovative tools, and cultivate a genuine passion for the field. To complement and enhance the academic curriculum, the association has planned an Inter-Collegiate Technical Competition titled “Techtonic – The Epicenter of Innovation”, exclusively for undergraduate Computer Science students from Arts and Science colleges in and around Chennai. The event will be held on September 12, 2025 at the Anderson Hall, Madras Christian College.

“Techtonic” symbolizes the powerful and transformative influence of technology on the world, suggesting a seismic shift and significant advancements in the tech industry. The event aims to explore and showcase groundbreaking innovations that are reshaping the technological landscape. Featuring six diverse technical events, it provides an excellent platform for you to exhibit, promote, and advertise your products.
